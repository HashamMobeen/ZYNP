ZYNP International is at the forefront of redefining automotive excellence through its commitment to innovation, advanced research and development, and state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities. We specialize in the production of premium propulsion components for gas, diesel, and electric vehicles. In addition, we have cutting-edge hydrogen fuel cell capabilities that cater to medium-to-heavy-duty OEMs, agricultural, and marine manufacturers alike. Our unwavering commitment to quality drives us to make dependable, cost-effective products that align with customer standards.


Located in Romulus, Michigan, our North American headquarters is home to talented sales and logistics teams, together with a specialized engineering team that is equipped with on-site laboratories. Originally founded in 1958 in Zhengzhou, ZYNP Corporation has built a legacy of product innovation, technological mastery, and exceptional value for our customers.
